About Us

This is a public limited company having its registered and corporate office at Butwal City of Lumbini State of Nepal. The company was incorporated in the year 2024 and production commenced in 2024. This is one of the fastest-growing public limited companies in Nepal.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality dairy products that nourish and delight our customers, while fostering sustainable growth, innovation, and social responsibility. We are committed to enhancing the health and well-being of our communities by ensuring the highest standards of quality and integrity in every product we offer.

Our Vision

To be the leading dairy brand in Nepal, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We aim to expand our reach and positively impact the lives of our consumers, employees, and the environment, becoming a symbol of trust and excellence in the dairy industry.
